History

Luuya R.C primary school is one of the oldest public schools in larger Bungoma district having been established in 1937 under the management of the curriculum from standard 1 to 4.In 1958 an intermediate section standard 5 to 8 (standard 5 to 8)was wholly transferred to the school from Kibabii intermediate school.The transfer was to give way to the then Kibabii Teachera college(TTC)The college exixted between 1958upto 1961,this college was also transfered to give way for present KibabiiBoys high school.The first class 8 ofLuuya primary therefore,sat for its Kenya African Prelimenary Examination(KAPE)in 1958.Ever since that time,theschool hasproduced many scholars who are today contributing immensely to the economicdevelopment of thecountry in various capacities.Among these personalities are the former member of parliament for sirisia constituency Hon.John Barasa Munyasia and the current vice chancellor of Masinde Muliro university of science and technology,Prof.B.C.C.Wangila,just to name a few. Also among its products is the present Luuya Girls High School.At its recent 70th anniversary,it is noted that the school has grown from a total pupil establishment of 20 pupilsin 1937 to 1037 in the year 2007 and 1210 pupils in the year 2011.
